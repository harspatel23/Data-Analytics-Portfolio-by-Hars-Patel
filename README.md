# Super Store Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Business Problem
The goal of this project is to analyze sales performance across different regions, product categories, customer segments, and time periods.  
This dashboard helps identify top-performing products, regional sales trends, and monthly sales patterns to support better business decisions.

## Dataset
- Source: Sample Superstore Dataset
- Data includes:
  - Orders
  - Sales
  - Profit
  - Product details
  - Region and customer segment
- Time period: 2014 â€“ 2017

## Dashboard Overview
The Excel dashboard provides interactive insights using slicers and charts:

- Monthly sales trend to understand seasonality
- Top 10 products by total sales
- Sales performance comparison across regions
- Filters for Year, Category, Region, and Segment
- Reset Filters button for easy interaction

## Key Insights
- West region generates the highest overall sales
- Sales peak during the later months of the year
- Technology and Office Supplies categories perform strongly
- A small number of products contribute a large share of total sales
